THEAR PARK-Theosophical Park

Theosophical Society Garden (Thear Park) is a serene and peaceful green space located in Adyar, Chennai. It is part of the Theosophical Society, an international organization dedicated to promoting universal brotherhood and the exploration of spiritual and philosophical ideas. Key Features: - Historic Trees:The park is home to a famous 450-year-old banyan tree, one of the largest in India. - Nature & Tranquility: The garden has a calm, tranquil atmosphere, with lush greenery, walking trails, and a variety of plant species. - Spiritual Significance: The park is associated with the Theosophical Society's teachings and serves as a place for meditation and reflection. - Wildlife: The garden is also home to birds and small wildlife, adding to its peaceful and natural charm.
